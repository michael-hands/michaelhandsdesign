Project Overview

Pleasure is temporary

An inspirational quote from the Dalai Lama. I think I picked this quote out from a book - The art of happiness. At this time I was going through a typographic stage and was learning some new techniques in illustrator to create crisp type. Also, I think I made this style this way because I also wanted to experiment with some 3D work. The block like structure seemed to make sense to transform into a 3D piece.
