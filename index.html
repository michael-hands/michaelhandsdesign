About me

About me

Hi! You can call me Mike. Last year I graduated in Graphic design & communication. I then decided to take a little me time, travelling -it was great, thanks- doing what I deemed best for me, struggling to decide where best to apply my skills.

The dream was to work solo, free-lance from the beach, but that I have figured is a dream for the future. So I changed my course. I am now seeking new life. I wish to be a sponge. I wish to learn from the many, the many like minded. To be part of the collective.

What I do

Skills

As an individual in the design world, I have taken a path where I tried to broaden my horizons in all areas of design. The experimental process is what I love most about it, I am still trying to discover an area in which I would love to specialise. Design is forever changing and I think it is impossible to tie myself to a certain area. Therefore, I have experience in many areas including: branding and logo, illustration, animation, 3D, print, typography/calligraphy, web/coding, photography etc...
