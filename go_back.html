Project Overview

Go back to nature

This piece was my entry for a D&AD brief. A rebelious period I may admit. Wanting to get away from the computer screen and inspired by the outdoors.

I am in love with nature and the beauty it brings to the world. It's just a shame that we are slowly losing touch with it. I created this piece as part of my own mini project to inspire people - like I had been from others - to reconnect with nature.

Following the recent calligraffiti movement closely, I was inspired by many artists projecting their expressions in the streets and also nature. However, these astonishing pieces were still mostly typically a 'graffiti' piece and a real message was not being conveyed. So I headed out into nature to leave a mark of my own
