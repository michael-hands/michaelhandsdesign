Project Overview

Owly

Little owly here was a creation of inspired boredom. I don't mean to upset him but thats just the way it is. Sometimes when I see some artists work I like to try to decypher how an illustrator creates their pieces. This was one of those times.

Although my attempt at the artists work was uncanny, as was expected before hand... I was pleased to experience a new style of work. Trying a limited colour palette and using gradients to create depth was my main goal. I had lots of fun with this piece and was very happy to have a cool little owl by the end of it.
